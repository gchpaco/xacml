/ : (num num num ... -> num)

purpose:
to divide the first by the second (and all following) number(s)
None but the first number can be zero.


Reductions:
(/ number_1 number_2 ...) --> the quotient of the numbers
   where each number_2 is not zero
(/ number_1 number_2 ...) e--> /: division by zero
   where at least one number_2 is zero
(/ v_arg ...) e--> /: expects type <number>
   where at least one v_arg is not a number
