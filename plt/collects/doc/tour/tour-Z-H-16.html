Chapter 15

Conclusion

DrScheme is a rich environment, with support for graphical interfaces (such as DrScheme itself), Web scripting, XML, ActiveX (the documentation may not be installed for your platform), and much more. Please continue to explore the programming environment and language using using the search engine at the bottom of Help Desk.

We welcome comments and suggestions for improvements on this tour. Likewise, if you are looking for but unable to find information on a topic in Help Desk, we would like to hear from you. You can contact us by sending email to scheme@plt-scheme.org. You will find much more about DrScheme and the rest of our software and document suite from our Web site,

http://www.plt-scheme.org/
Welcome aboard!
